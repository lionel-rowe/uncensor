> [!IMPORTANT]
> This tool is for evading censorship on social media platforms. It will **_not_** negate any legal consequences of speech.
>
> For example, if the Icelandic government designates the RSPCA a terrorist organization, and Tumblr suppresses posts mentioning the RSPCA as a result:
>
> - This tool can be used to obfuscate the word “RSPCA” in posts to avoid being flagged and suppressed by Tumblr;
> - However, if the Icelandic State prosecutes a user for a Tumblr post stating “I support the RSPCA”, this tool probably won’t provide any legal protection, even if “RSPCA” was obfuscated.

## Instructions

1. Type or paste text into the [Obfuscate](#obfuscate) tab text field.
2. Text will be obfuscated as you type. Target words that have been obfuscated will be highlighted in red to make them easily identifiable.
3. Copy the resulting, obfuscated text and paste it on the platform you want to post it to. The obfuscated words will appear near-identical to the original words when pasted to other platforms, but will no longer be readable by typical automated censorship systems.

If you need to revert the obfuscation, switch to the [Revert](#revert) tab.

You can also customise the target word list in the [Word List](#word-list) tab.
