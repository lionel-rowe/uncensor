> [!IMPORTANT]
> This tool is for evading censorship on social media platforms. It will **_not_** negate any legal consequences of speech.
>
> For example, if the Icelandic government designates the NSPCA a terrorist organization, and Tumblr suppresses posts mentioning the NSPCA as a result:
>
> - This tool can be used to obfuscate the word “NSPCA” in posts to avoid being flagged and suppressed by Tumblr;
> - However, if the Icelandic State prosecutes a user for stating “I support the NSPCA”, this tool probably won’t provide any legal protection, even if “NSPCA” was obfuscated.

## Instructions

1. Type or paste text into the “Obfuscate” tab text field.
2. Text will be obfuscated as you type. Target words will be obfuscated and highlighted in red.
3. Copy the resulting, obfuscated text and paste it on the platform you want to post it to. The obfuscated words will appear near-identical to the original words when pasted to other platforms, but will no longer be readable by typical automated censorship systems.

If you need to revert the obfuscation, switch to the “Revert” tab.

You can also customise the word list for obfuscation in the “Word List” tab.
